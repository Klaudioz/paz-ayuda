Privacy Policy for Wterapia

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wterapia ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our WhatsApp-based therapy bot service. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you interact with Wterapia, we collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Profile Information: Name associated with your WhatsApp account
  • Onboarding Information: Age, gender identity, and description of your concerns
  • Communication Content: Messages, voice notes, and images you send to our bot
  • Voice Preferences: Your preference for male or female voice responses

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Frequency and duration of interactions, types of messages sent
  • Technical Information: Device information, WhatsApp version
  • Location Information: Country and time zone (as provided by WhatsApp)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Personalize Services: To deliver our therapy bot services and personalize your experience
  • Improve Our Services: To analyze interactions and enhance the effectiveness of our bot
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Research and Development: To develop new features and improve existing ones
  • Safety and Security: To verify accounts and activities, and prevent misuse

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure with enterprise-grade security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Distributed storage to prevent single points of failure

Your conversation history is stored for a period of 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted unless you request deletion sooner.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us deliver our services (e.g., AI providers, analytics tools)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or government requests
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Wterapia, our users, or others

6. AI Processing

Our service utilizes artificial intelligence technologies from third-party providers:

  • OpenAI: For processing audio and image content
  • Google Gemini: For conversation processing and responses

These services process your message content to generate responses. While these providers may temporarily process your data, they do not retain or use your personal conversations for training their models.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Discontinue use of our service at any time

To exercise these rights, send a message to our bot with "/ayuda derechos" for guidance on the process.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us to have the information deleted.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in compliance with applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website and sending a notification through the WhatsApp bot. Your continued use of the service after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@wterapia.com
WhatsApp: Send "/contacto" to our bot

By using Wterapia, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.
