Data Deletion Instructions for Wterapia

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Your Right to Data Deletion

At Wterapia, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This document outlines the process for requesting deletion of your personal information from our systems.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Through the WhatsApp Bot

  1. Send the message "/borrar datos" to our WhatsApp number
  2. The bot will ask you to confirm your decision
  3. Reply with "CONFIRMAR" to proceed with deletion
  4. You will receive a confirmation message once your data has been deleted

Option 2: Via Email

  1. Send an email to privacy@wterapia.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your WhatsApp phone number in the email
  3. We will process your request within 5 business days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • All personal information collected during onboarding (name, age, gender)
  • Your conversation history with the bot
  • Voice preferences and settings
  • Any media files you have shared (audio messages, images)
  • Your phone number from our database

Retention for Legal Purposes

Please note that we may be required to retain certain information for legal or regulatory purposes, such as:

  • Records of your deletion request
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics
  • Information required for legal compliance

This retained information will not be used to contact you or provide services.

Third-Party Data

While we can delete all data stored in our systems, please be aware that:

  1. WhatsApp: Messages sent through WhatsApp may still be stored according to WhatsApp's own data retention policies. Please consult WhatsApp's privacy policy for more information.
  2. AI Providers: While we ensure our AI service providers (OpenAI, Google) do not retain your personal conversations, you would need to contact these providers directly regarding any concerns about their data practices.

Effect on Your Service

After your data is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to use the Wterapia service with the same phone number
  • The bot will no longer have any record of your previous conversations
  • If you contact the service again, you will be treated as a new user

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized deletion requests, we verify all deletion requests by:

  1. Confirming the request comes from the same WhatsApp number in our records
  2. Requiring explicit confirmation before proceeding
  3. For email requests, sending a verification code to your WhatsApp number

Contact for Assistance

If you encounter any issues with the data deletion process or have questions about your data, please contact our Data Protection team:

  • Email: privacy@wterapia.com
  • Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
  • WhatsApp: Send "/contacto soporte" to our bot

We are committed to handling your request promptly and transparently.

Wterapia respects your data privacy rights and strives to make the data deletion process as simple and straightforward as possible.
